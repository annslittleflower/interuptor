Interuptor

Extension designed to improve your health by bringing you back from work or spiral of useless content you actually don't need to watch

Periodically you will be notified to:

  • drink some water
  • stop scrolling feed
  • move
  • stretch
  • look out the window
  • lie down for a bit
  • listen to you own thoughts
